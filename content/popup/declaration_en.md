This is a non-profit fan community platform dedicated to collecting and showcasing fan-created artwork inspired by *Zootopia*.

## Content Policy & Reporting

This site is committed to maintaining an **all-ages** browsing environment.

- NSFW policy: We do not display any pornographic, excessively violent, or otherwise inappropriate content (NSFW). All content is reviewed before being published.
- Reporting: If you find any content on this site that violates our policy, is offensive, or has been incorrectly rated, please notify us immediately at lora@zootopia.gallery. We commit to reviewing and permanently removing the content within 24 hours of receiving your report.

## Reposting & Attribution Policy

We deeply respect the work of every creator:

- Source attribution: Each artwork is clearly labeled with the original artist's handle and the platform where it was first published.
- Non-commercial: This site contains no advertisements, charges no fees, and does not monetize any of the displayed works in any way.
- Takedown requests: If you are the rights holder of a work and do not wish it to appear on this site, please contact us at lora@zootopia.gallery. Upon verification, we will remove the content within 24–48 hours.

We encourage everyone to visit the original platforms and support the artists directly.
